Currency

The name of the Vietnamese currency is đồng. The smaller monetary units xu (1/10 of Vietnamese đồng) and hào (1/100 of Vietnamese đồng) are rarely used. The smallest banknote denomination is 100 đồng, and the biggest is 500,000 đồng.
